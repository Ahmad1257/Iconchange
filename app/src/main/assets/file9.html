Q#:9.1. The concrete roof of a house of thickness 20cm has an area 200m2. The temperature inside the house is 15oC and outside is 35oC. Find the rate at which thermal energy will be conducted through the roof. The value of k for concrete is 0.65W m-1K-1

Ans.Solution:

Given data:

                      L   =   2cm=20/100=0.2m                      
                      A   =   200m2 

                       T1 =    35oC

                            = 35 + 273 = 308K

                      T2 = 15oC

                           = 15 + 273 = 288K

                     ∆T = T1 – T2

                          = 308 – 288 = 20K

                     ∆T = 20K

Thermal conductivity = k = 0.65 Wm-1K-1

To Find:

Rate of flow = Q / t = ?

Solution:

                  Q         kA(T1−T2)          kA∆T

                           = ______________
