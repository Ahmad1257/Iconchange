Q#:3.1. A force of 20N moves a body with an acceleration of 2ms-2, what is its mass?

Ans.Solution:

Given data:

                    F = 20N

                    a = 2ms-2

To Find:

                   m = ?

Solution:

By using Newton’s second law of motion.

                   F = ma

                  m = F / a

                  m = 20 / 2

                  m = 10kg
